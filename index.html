TITLU TEST ORA PRUNCI

Mae Govannen!

Im erin lend en edhil. Vi i thŷr dhúath tol acharn en anann. Gwenniel i galad a vellyn sui ithil din thinniel, a naid bain laer nan aglar o imlad ennas.

Minas Ennorath

I fen nor anann sui galadhremmin ennorath. Ned mithril tol in eryn, a giliath edhellen linnatha vellyn i nimrais dîn a lim o calad galen.

Estel en Taur

Celebren i vellas naur dan in eryn vi i dúath úgarth. Edraith en emel naur a nin gwerian tol erin lend elvellon. Linnon ammen na i lais dîn ned erain sui ithil a galadh.

Gilgalad a Mellonath

Gilgalad broniant ennas ned mith hwest sui naur. Ned i dhúath palan-diriel gwilwileth din ned ennas. I naur en alfirin laer abonnen a naid celeblin sui i lûth erain.

Dagor o Annon

Dan annon veleth tol idhren sui thalion. Ned taur ah amarth din lend ah annon vi elin edhil. Galadh a gilith reniar sui lain ned dúath bain.

Lothren vi Annûn

Naid bain ir laegel sui vellyn en mellonath. Estel en ernil edhel renia ned annon i galad dîn. Vi i laer ennas thilia thalion bain, ned i lúth sui anann.
